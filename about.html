Halle Northrup About Me

Hi! My name is Halle Northrup, and I am from Aurora, Colorado. I am currently at Brigham Young University, majoring in Experience Design and Management with a minor in Family Life. I am an avid rock climber, airplane and flying enthusiast, full-time child wrangler, and amateur wedding cake baker. I have been climbing for most of my life, and I am now the girls' coach of the Provo Climbing Team. I also received my Commercial Pilot License in December 2025 and am working toward becoming a corporate pilot. I work as a nanny for two little boys, and also take on side jobs, like cleaning houses and making wedding cakes!

Have fun and be safe!
