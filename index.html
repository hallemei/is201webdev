Learning To Rock Climb

Climbing is arguably one of the best sports in the world. You get to climb big rocks, swing around on ropes like Spider-Man, wear cute clothes, and look really strong to people who do not climb.

What You'll Need

  • Gear
    1. Harness
    2. Shoes
    3. Chalk
    4. Belay device
  • A willingness to learn through a few falls and failed attempts
  • A reliable belay buddy and a good attitude
